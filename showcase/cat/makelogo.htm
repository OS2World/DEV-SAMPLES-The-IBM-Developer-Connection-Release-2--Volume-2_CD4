OS/2 BootLogo Utility-Volume 6 (M4)

This product is located on Member Disc 4.

The MAKELOGO utility program (available in the "Bitmaps" category) helps you create your own boot logo screens. Two sample boot logos and corresponding intermediate (VRAM) files are provided.

Refer to "Personalize Your OS/2 Warp Boot Screen" in The Developer Connection News, Volume 6, for a detailed description of how you can create your own personalized boot logo screen.

Installation Instructions:

To install from the CD:

  1. Copy the X:\makelogo directory to your hard drive. (X represents your CD-ROM drive.)
  2. Refer to the above article for more information.
