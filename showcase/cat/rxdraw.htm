Extend Your Programs with REXX-Volume 6 (M4)

This product is located on Member Disc 4.

RXDRAW is a sample Presentation Manager program to accompany the article "Extend Your Programs with REXX" in The Developer Connection News, Volume 6. This sample is intended for any developer considering a macro language based on REXX for their application program.

RXDRAW calls REXX as a subroutine to draw simple graphics in its main window.

Installation Instructions:

To install from the CD:

  1. Copy the X:\rxdraw directory on the CD to your hard drive. (X represents your CD-ROM drive.)
  2. Refer to the above article for more information.
