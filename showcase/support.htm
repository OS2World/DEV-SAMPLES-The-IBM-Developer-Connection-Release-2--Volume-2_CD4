Support for the IBM Developer Connection

For questions about your IBM Developer Connection subscription status, renewals, or changing your address, call the appropriate ordering phone number for your country. This information is presented when you press the OrderInfo link on the first page of the catalog.

You can reset your password, change your password or update your e-mail address in our database from our Web site at www.developer.ibm.com/devcon/ Click on Subscriber Services to access the DevCon Online User ID and Password Management Services. This option applies only to your Developer Connection user ID and password.

Support for the products contained on the Developer Connection CDs may vary; some have no support, others have varying levels of free and fee-based support. For questions about specific products on the CDs, refer to the information in the online catalog when you select that product entry, or refer to the individual product readme files. You also can check the related URLs below, or those in the WebLinks area, for additional support options.

For questions about specific operating system platforms or extensions, refer to the operating system product information for the operating system technical support methods in your country.

Technical support for the IBM Developer Connection is available through the Internet. Developer Connection support personnel will resolve "How To" questions and problems with the Developer Connection interface, installation or function. Questions and comments can be sent to us via e-mail to devcon@us.ibm.com.

You also can exchange messages, ideas, or comments with the IBM Developer Connection team using the DEVCON CFORUM under TalkLink on the OS/2 BBS, which is a feature under the IBMLink Commercial Services.

For information about TalkLink access, U.S. customers can call 1-800-547-1283. Customers outside of the U.S. should contact their local IBM Marketing Representative.
